This repo will hold the MCAL drivers needed to activate all the peripherals in Avr Atmega32 MCU.

Drivers:
- DIO.
- Interrupt.
- ADC.
- Analog Comparator.
- Timer/Counter 0.
- Timer/Counter 1.
- Timer/Counter 2.
- PWM.
- SPI.
- USART.
- TWI.
- EEPROM.
- FLASH.
- Power Management.
- Watchdog Timer.
